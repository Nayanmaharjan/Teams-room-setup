# Microsoft Teams Room Setup (Onsite)

## Overview
This project documents the on-site setup of a Microsoft Teams Room for a business client.  
The purpose of the setup was to create a reliable, professional meeting room environment using Microsoft Teams, with clean cabling, stable connectivity, and properly configured audio and video devices.

This repository focuses on the **practical ICT support work** involved in setting up and validating a Teams Room environment rather than software development.

---

## Scope of Work
- On-site setup of a Microsoft Teams Room
- Connection and configuration of display, mini PC, camera, microphone, and speakers
- Verification of audio, video, and display quality
- Basic network connectivity checks
- Final testing to ensure the room was ready for business use

---

## Hardware & Components
- Sony Smart TV (display)
- Windows-based mini PC
- Yamaha CS-800 video soundbar (camera, microphone, speaker â€“ Microsoft Teams certified)
- HDMI cabling between mini PC and TV
- Wired network connection

---

## Configuration & Setup
- Positioned the display to ensure clear visibility and a professional presentation
- Connected the Windows-based mini PC to the Sony Smart TV using HDMI
- Routed and tucked in all cabling neatly to avoid clutter and maintain a clean finish
- Configured Windows and Microsoft Teams to recognise the Yamaha CS-800 correctly:
  - Camera input
  - Microphone input
  - Audio output
- Verified correct screen resolution and display scaling on the TV
- Ensured the system was stable and ready for testing

---

## Validation & Testing
- Joined test Microsoft Teams meetings to confirm:
  - Video output displayed correctly on the TV
  - Camera feed from the Yamaha CS-800 was clear and stable
  - Microphone and speaker audio worked as expected
- Performed basic network connectivity checks
- Confirmed the final setup provided a clean, clutter-free display with reliable audio and video performance

---

## Outcome
The Teams Room was successfully set up with a clean and professional finish.  
All devices were correctly configured and tested, providing a reliable meeting room environment suitable for day-to-day business meetings.
